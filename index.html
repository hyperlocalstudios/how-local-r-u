How Local R U?

Calculate your L.O.C.A.L. variables and get your local score (0–10, 10 being the most local). It's like a Buzzfeed quiz, but nerdier and infinitely more tedious!

01LIFESPAN

How long have you been living in this place?

While you do get a small boost for being able to claim heritage somewhere, we think time in a place is one of the most important driving factors for being a local.
02OUTREACH

How about town are you?

Excludes work/school. Only count activities within your local area.

Parks, gyms, trails, sports leagues, yoga studios

Restaurants, cafes, bars, farmers markets

Thrifting, going to the movies, concerts, art markets

Clubs, meetups, community centers, classes, religious services

Volunteering, local government meetings, community organizing

This variable rewards both how often you engage in local activities and the diversity of your engagement across different categories. Being active in multiple categories shows you're well-integrated into your community.

04COMPASS

How good is your lay of the land?

Where 1 is "not really" and 5 is "completely."

  • I can give directions without my phone
  • I know lots of shortcuts
  • I can orient myself based on local landmarks
  • I regularly navigate without GPS
  • I can name most neighborhoods/boroughs
This variable is self-reported based on your spatial understanding of neighborhood boundaries, navigation, and internal "compass."
05AUTHENTICITY

This is your proof of life — do you have a read on the local vibes? What are the neighborhood hot takes?

  • I know at least one local gripe or have my own neighborhood rant
  • I can claim I went to X spot when it was still Y
  • I'm regular enough somewhere that someone who works there knows my name
  • I've personally introduced someone to a local tradition
  • I know a local joint or event a tourist would never find on Yelp or IG reels
This is another self-reported variable based on your closeness to the issues that plague your neighborhood. Think: "When is construction on that lot finally going to end?" "I can't believe they haven't fixed that traffic light yet!" "I wonder what's going to open where that business closed..."

about the equation
The function that underlies your score is a logistic growth curve, a mathematical model that produces a distinctive S-shaped (sigmoid) graph, commonly used to describe growth, spread, or probability that starts exponentially but slows down and eventually levels off as it reaches a maximum limiting value.
about local archetype
Your archetype is assigned based on your years lived, whether you were born in the area, your civic engagement patterns, how often you visit local places, and your knowledge of local lore.
